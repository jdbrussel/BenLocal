# Filament Admin Documentation

## Trust & Reputation Dashboard

Moderators can monitor the health of the trust engine through new widgets and views.

### Monitoring Widgets
- **Top Hidden Gems:** Real-time list of spots with the highest hidden gem scores.
- **Rising Spots:** Spots with the fastest growing local trust.
- **Suspicious Patterns:** Flags spots with high recommendation counts but low verified visit ratios.

### User Reputation Management
The User Resource now includes a "Reputation" tab:
- View trust scores per region/category.
- Inspect trust penalty history.
- Manually adjust reputation for verified local experts.

### Spot Score Inspection
The Spot Resource shows calculated metrics:
- Local vs Tourist balance.
- Community influence breakdown.
- Audit log of score recalculations.

## Owner Panel (`/owner`)

A dedicated panel for business owners and managers.

### Resources
- **My Spots:** Edit contact info, opening hours, and photos.
- **Reviews:** View and respond to customer reviews.

## Admin Panel - Business Management

### Resources
- **Spot Claims:** Manage the queue of business claim requests.
- **Claim Tokens:** Generate and track onboarding tokens.
- **Owner Roles:** Manage which users have access to which business dashboard.
